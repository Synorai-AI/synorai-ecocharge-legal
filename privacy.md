# Synorai EcoCharge Privacy Policy

Synorai EcoCharge (“the App”) helps Shopify merchants apply environmental handling fee adjustments to cart line items.

## Data Collection
The App does not collect or store customer personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

The App may access limited Shopify store data only to provide its core functionality, including:

- Product tags (to determine fee categories)
- Merchant-selected compliance province settings
- Cart line item information (to apply fee adjustments)

## Data Sharing
Synorai EcoCharge does not sell or share merchant or customer data with third parties.

## Merchant Responsibility
Merchants remain responsible for all environmental fee compliance, reporting, and remittance obligations.
